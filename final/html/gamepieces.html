Game Pieces

Here are the different game pieces you have read about in the rules!

Train Pieces!

These are what the trains look like, each train pieces gets played with a train car. The color of train pieces corresponds with the PLAYER not the cards and board.

Train Cards!

These are your train cards, they are what you are collecting through out your turns in order to play your train pieces. Each color corresponds with the color of the path on the game board NOT the plastic train cars.

Destination Tickets!

These are your destination tickets. They show what two cities you need to connect in order to redeem the points that are on the card. Don't forget, if you fail to complete the destination ticket, you will get those points deducted from your final score!
